Dan Marino

The best quarterback the Miami Dolphins ever had!

Daniel Constantine Marino Jr.

Played in the NFL for 17 seasons with the Miami Dolphins. He has held or current holds dozens of NFL records, despite never winning the Super Bowl. He is recognized among the greates quartebacks in American football history! He is Best remembered for his quick relase and powerful arm.

He is a nine-time Pro Bowl selection, six-time first or second team All-Pro, and All-AFC six times. Marino won all three major individual awards: NFL MVP (1984), NFL Comeback Player of the Year (1994), and NFL Man of the Year (1998).

When he finally retired, Marino held more than 40 NFL single-season and career passing recods!
